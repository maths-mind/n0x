Instructions

Stunt Bike Extreme takes motorcycle stunts to a whole new level with its stunning graphics that stand out among browser games! Get ready for progressive gameplay that challenges you with each level, introducing increasingly difficult obstacles and ramps that test your skills. Choose from multiple game modes, including the exhilarating option to race against real players, adding an exciting competitive edge. Master flips and tricks to earn points while navigating through beautifully designed environments like dense jungles and industrial warehouses. With each successful jump, you can unlock upgrades and customization options for your bike. Are you prepared to dominate the stunt bike scene?

How to play Stunt Bike Extreme?

  • Use the up and down arrow keys or pedal icons to accelerate and brake.
  • Use the left and right arrow keys or the icons in the bottom left to execute flips.

Who created Stunt Bike Extreme?

Stunt Bike Extreme is created by Hyperkani. This is their first game on Playtropolis!
